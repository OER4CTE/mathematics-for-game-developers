Unit 1: Some Basic Algebra

Students learn basic algebra concepts which include constants, variables, and expressions. There are examples to practice, and even an example with Wolfram Alpha, a coding program with mathematics language.

  • Full Project

Unit 2: Vectors in Two Dimensions

In this unit, students learn the basics of vectors, such as the standard position, row and column forms, and equal vectors. There is also other lessons as well:
-addition, subtraction and scalar multiplication of vectors
-the magnitude, direction and components of a vector
-dot product of two vectors, length of vector, and angle between two vectors
-parallel, perpendicular, and unit vectors
vector projection

  • Full Project

Unit 3: Vectors in Three Dimensions

The vector rules in Unit 2 are further expanded on to a three-dimensional space. We learn the distance between two points in 2D and 3D and the equation of a circle and a sphere. There are also these additional lessons:
-magnitude and direction cosines of a vector
-addition, subtraction, and scalar multiplication of 3D vectors
-unit 3D vectors, normalization, and standard positions
-dot product, length, and angle
-cross product and right-hand rule using algebra, -cross product and right-hand rule with geometry, parallelogram area, perpendicular and parallel cross product

  • Full Project

Unit 4: Matrices

Students are introduced to matrices. They learn the dimensions and elements of a matrix, the equal and square matrices, tthe main diagonal of a matrix, the identity and zero matrix, the transpose of a matrix, row and column matrices, and vectors as matrices. Additional lessons include:
-addition, subtraction, scalar multiplication, and products of row and column matrices
-compatible matrices, matrices as a collection of row and column matrices, and matrix multiplication
-rotation of matrices in 2D
-angle of rotation in 2D
-rotating matrices in 3D

  • Full Project

Unit 5: Some Basic Trigonometry

Students learn basic trigonometry rules such as the sine, cosine and tangent of angles. There are other lessons included:
-circular trigonometry
-sine function graph
-cosine function graph
-amplitude and period
